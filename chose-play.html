You felt curious about how to use the stick, so you ate the sweets with your hands, and then started to think how the stick can be used.

As you think, you came up of an image of an orchestra concert you had recently watched on TV, and imagined yourself being the conductor, and your stick being a taktstock.

As you continue to imagine, you feel a brand new music of your own coming up to your mind!

You conduct the orchestra in front of you, sometime gently, sometimes with great passion... Your band beautifully performs the music...

After you have ended your performance, you hear a great applause...!

After you have enjoyed the tea ceremony, you introduced the music that came up in your imagination, and the music became the greatest hit of the decade!

You continued to introduce your music with your wonderful inspiration, and became a great composer!
